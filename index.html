Our Work

Reducing Cost, Emissions and local Footprint

Technology involves drilling holes at predetermine intervals. Injecting steam and air using the injection holes and taking the gas out using production holes. The gas can be burnt in existing boiler but best use is in igcc engine.

Advantages of UCG

UCG has numerous advantages over conventional underground or strip mining and surface gasification, including:

Eco-friendly

Conventional coal mining is eliminated with UCG, reducing operating costs, surface damage and eliminating mine safety issues such as mine collapse and asphyxiation;

Sustanbility

No surface gasification, capital costs substantially reduced

Worker's Safety

Coals that are unmineable (too deep, low grade, thin seams) are exploitable by UCG, greatly increasing domestic resource availability

Cost cutting

No coal is transported at the surface, reducing cost, emissions, and local Footprint associated with coal shipping and stockpiling.
